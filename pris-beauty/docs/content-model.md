# PRIS Beauty Content Model

## brand

- `name`
- `positioning`
- `backing`
- `summary`

## contact

- `phoneDisplay`
- `whatsappNumber`
- `whatsappPrefill`
- `email`
- `address`
- `hours`
- `mapUrl`

## hero

- `title`
- `subtitle`

## services

- 3 to 5 facial-first service cards

## concerns

- 4 to 6 common skin concern entry points

## testimonials

- `quote`
- `source`

## faq

- `question`
- `answer`

## assets

- `name`
- `source`
- `intendedUse`

## Notes

- This model is intentionally simple so the site can expand into multi-page or bilingual versions later.
- Public business facts should remain source-backed.
- Any non-verified claims should be excluded or clearly marked for confirmation.
